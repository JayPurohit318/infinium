Solution

What we Do?

Improve your processes, serve more customers, and grow your business with cutting-edge technology.

Intelligently Reduce Risks, Costs and the "DUMB" (Data Under Management Budget)

A standout feature of 4iG is its ability to index, perform OCR, and process data in its original location. Unlike other "IG" solutions, 4iG allows seamless actions on data without the need to duplicate or move data to another location or 3rd party cloud.

Directly Connect to Structured & Unstructured Data

With 50+ data connectors covering a range of legacy and modern sources (such as MSO365, GSuite, Teams, Slack, Salesforce), supplemented by custom-built connectors as per client needs.

Robust Compliance Framework

4iG includes a repository of 600+ PII profiles categorized by country, type, and policy, alongside 1500+ customizable retention policies.

Comprehensive Built-in Solutions

Offering an array of built-in solutions encompassing data disposition, compliance monitoring, legal hold, PII redaction, entity identification (ProfilerAI), breach investigation/review, eDiscovery, early case assessment (ECA), federated search, analytics, and risk assessment.

Template-based Automation

Facilitating template creation and automation for swift, repeatable and defensible deployment of solutions.
